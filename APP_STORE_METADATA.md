## Full Description

**Turn your dreams into reality with Stepora** — the intelligent goal-setting app that combines AI coaching, structured planning, and social accountability.

**Plan with AI Intelligence**
Share your dream and our AI coach creates a personalized step-by-step plan with milestones, tasks, and check-in schedules tailored to your pace.

**Track Your Progress**
Daily check-ins, streak tracking, and visual analytics keep you motivated and on course. See your progress come to life with vision boards and milestone celebrations.

**Grow Together**
Connect with accountability buddies, join dream circles, compete in leagues, and share your journey. Stepora makes achieving goals a social experience.

**Stay Motivated**
XP rewards, achievements, league rankings, and a virtual store keep you engaged. Unlock badges, themes, and decorations as you progress.

**Key Features:**
- AI-powered dream analysis and plan generation
- Smart calendar with task scheduling
- Daily check-ins and streak tracking
- Accountability buddies with matching
- Dream circles and community
- Vision boards with AI-generated images
- Leagues and leaderboards
- Virtual store with XP rewards
- Focus mode and productivity tools
- Dark glass morphism design
